                            SSSO Pillars

                            Our programs empower members to engage in their communities and become the leaders of tomorrow. Our pillars lift youth to new heights.

                            Education

                            Our team’s backgrounds in education and passion for working with youth drives us to give students the academic support they need to be successful, by providing services and resources such as tutoring, homework and exam help, and financial literacy.

                            Read More

                            Community
                            Outreach

                            Teaching the youngest members of our community how to care for those around them fosters lifelong leadership. Our events and programs inspire youth to be of service to their communities and neighbours by providing them with the chance to give back.

                            Read More

                            Athletics

                            Sports have the power to support classroom learning, and we want to grant access to a range of sports for all children aged 5-17. Our athletics programs give them a safe space to get physically active, make connections with peers & learn valuable skills such as collaboration, discipline and problem-solving.

                            Read More

                            Mental Health
                            Initiatives

                            We believe that a healthy mind assures youth success. From our Mental Health Programs to our Anxiety 101 workshop our objectives are to ensure the physical, mental and emotional health of our youth and their families. We will always keep the mental health of our members at the core of everything we do.
